Rajesh Krishna Khanna & Associates

Rajesh Krishna Khanna & Associates is a reknowned Category 1 CA firm in practice since 1987. Rajesh Krishna Khanna & Associates provides a range of accounting, auditing, taxation and consultancy services to individuals and businesses. With over 35 years of experience and expertise, the firm aims to deliver high-quality solutions that meet the needs and expectations of its clients. The firm has a team of qualified and experienced professionals who are committed to excellence, integrity and ethics. Whether you need assistance with income tax returns, GST compliance, financial statements, internal audits, business valuation, or any other accounting or financial matter, Rajesh Krishna Khanna & Associates is your trusted partner. Contact us today to find out how we can help you achieve your goals.

Rajesh Krishna Khanna & Associates that operates in New Delhi & Gurugram serves clients across India and abroad. The firm has a strong reputation for delivering timely and accurate services that comply with the latest laws and regulations.

About Us

We follow a client-centric approach and a collaborative culture. We assign a dedicated team of professionals to each client, who work closely with them to understand their requirements, challenges, and goals. The team then devises customized solutions that suit the client's specific needs and preferences. The team also maintains regular communication with the client, updating them on the progress and status of their work, and seeking their feedback and suggestions. The team also coordinates with other departments and experts within the firm, such as tax, audit, legal, or IT, to ensure that the client receives comprehensive and integrated services.
We also invest in training and development of our staff, keeping them updated on the latest trends and developments in the accounting and financial domain. We also foster a culture of innovation, creativity, and excellence, encouraging its staff to explore new ideas and methods to enhance the quality and efficiency of their work. Our's is a CA firm that strives to create value for its clients and stakeholders through its professional and ethical services.
